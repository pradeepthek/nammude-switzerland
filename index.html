About Nammude Switzerland

Built by the community,
for the community

Nammude Switzerland — "our Switzerland" — was born from a simple idea: Malayalees in Switzerland deserve a shared home online. A place to find each other, coordinate events without clashes, and keep the warmth of Kerala alive in the Alps.

We are a volunteer-run platform, independent and non-political, serving all Malayalees regardless of which organisation they belong to.

Inclusive — open to every Malayalee and family in Switzerland
Volunteer-run — built and maintained by community members
Non-political — a neutral space for all, serving no agenda
