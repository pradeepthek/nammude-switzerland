Privacy Policy

How Nammude Switzerland handles your information

Effective date:  May 13, 2026  ·  Applies to the Nammude Switzerland app & website
1

Who We Are

Nammude Switzerland (നമ്മുടെ സ്വിറ്റ്സർലൻഡ്) is a volunteer-run community platform for Malayalees living in Switzerland. We provide an events calendar, community board, and resources directory to help members stay connected and informed.

This Privacy Policy applies to both our website at nammude-switzerland.netlify.app and our Android app available on Google Play.

2

What Data We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide when submitting an event through our event submission form.

DataWhy we collect itRequired
Event nameTo display the event on the calendarYes
Event date & timeTo schedule and list the event correctlyYes
Event locationTo inform community members where to attendYes
Event descriptionTo provide context and details about the eventYes
Organiser name / contactFor admin review and follow-up if neededYes
Event categoryTo filter and categorise events correctlyYes
We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, device identifiers, location data, or any other personal data beyond what you choose to enter in the event submission form.
3

How We Use Your Data

Information submitted through the event form is used solely to:

  • Review and approve events before publishing them to the community calendar
  • Display approved events publicly on the Nammude Switzerland platform
  • Contact the organiser if clarification is needed about the submission

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond managing the community events calendar.

4

Data Storage

Submitted event data is stored in a Google Sheet managed by the Nammude Switzerland team. Google's infrastructure is used to process and store this data, and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Approved and published events become publicly visible on the platform. Rejected or pending submissions are retained for administrative review only and are not made public.

5

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Google Analytics — Used to understand how visitors use our website (page views, traffic sources). This may collect anonymised usage data. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Google Sheets & Apps Script — Used as our backend to receive, store, and manage event submissions.
  • Netlify — Our website hosting provider. Netlify may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, request times) as part of normal hosting operations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for commercial purposes.

6

Cookies

Our website may use cookies set by Google Analytics to track anonymous usage statistics. No other cookies are set by us. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use the platform.

7

Children's Privacy

Nammude Switzerland is a general community platform not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal data through our platform, please contact us so we can remove it promptly.

8

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data you have submitted
  • Request correction or deletion of your submitted event data
  • Withdraw consent for your event to be displayed publicly

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the platform after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, reach out to us.
